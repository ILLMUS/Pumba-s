Behind the Lens

I'm Lindani Dlamini aka Pumba, a visual storyteller from Mbabane, Eswatini. Through the lens of my camera, I capture not just images — but emotion, energy, and essence. My work blends documentary realism with cinematic flair.

🎥 What I Do

  • 📸 High-end Photography (Portraits, Brands, Events)
  • 🎬 Cinematic Videography (Weddings, Documentaries, Ads)
  • 💻 Video Editing
  • 🏙️ Location Scouting & Creative Direction

💡 My Creative Mission

I help individuals, couples, and brands in Eswatini and beyond tell their stories in powerful visuals. My craft is rooted in patience, precision, and passion — every shoot is a collaboration, every frame a legacy.

🌍 Based in Mbabane, Shooting Nationwide

From intimate weddings in Mbabane to creative shoots in Manzini — I’m mobile, equipped, and ready to capture your story anywhere in Southern Africa.
